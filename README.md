# wild-jam-93

## Game Concept
You are the owner of a failing rock shop. A recent meteor shower has provided a unique opportunity to collect, prepare and deliver meteorites to prospective buyers to save your business.

Overcooked/Papa's pizzeria-ish game where you collect meteorites, process them according to customer orders, and deliver them back to your shop before the sun rises.
